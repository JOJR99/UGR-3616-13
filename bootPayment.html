Payment method

  • Upon successful creation of the Pay Later Instalment Payment Plan, Cardmember will be billed the first month instalment amount in his /her monthly credit card statement.
  • Subsequently, Cardmember will be billed the instalment amount on a monthly basis until the end of the repayment period.
  • The Cardmember’s Credit Limit will be progressively restored with the amount of instalment payments received by the Bank.
  • If any instalment payment that was due in Cardmember’s monthly credit card statement and is not received by CBE Bank in full by the payment due date, the prevailing late fees and/or finance/interest charges on the outstanding amount(s) will apply.

Fees and charges

  • Cardmembers who choose to cancel their existing instalment plan will be charged an early termination fee of 2000 br /per Pay Later Instalment Payment Plan.
  • In the event that the CBE Credit Card is terminated by Cardmember, any Pay Later Instalment Payment Plan that is currently financed through the Card will be accelerated and made payable immediately. Additionally, an early termination fee of 2000 br will be levied for each Pay Later Instalment Payment Plan being terminated.
  • In the event of a merchant-refund on the original transaction, Cardmember’s Pay Later Instalment Payment Plan will not be automatically terminated by the Bank. Cardmember would need to contact and instruct the Bank to terminate the instalment plan if he/she wishes to do so. Early termination fee will be waived for merchant-refunded transactions.
  • Customer will have to ensure that the merchant-refund have been credited into the respective CBE Credit Card account before instructing the Bank to cancel instalment plan. If any outstanding instalment payment that was due in Cardmember’s monthly credit card statement and is not received by CBE Bank in full by the payment due date, Cardmember will be liable for the prevailing late fees and/or finance/interest charges on the outstanding amount(s)."
